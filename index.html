About The Sables Trust

Our Mission

The Sables Trust is a Zimbabwean entity established in 2017 to specifically support the national men's rugby team, known as the Sables. We focus on ensuring the team has the necessary resources, support, and infrastructure to compete at the highest levels of international rugby.

Our efforts have contributed significantly to the team's recent achievements, including their recognition at the Annual National Sports Awards (ANSA), where the Sables team and coach, Piet Benade, were awarded.

The Trust is deeply involved in the team's preparations for the Rugby Africa Cup and the World Cup qualification process, providing the foundation for success on the international stage.

Key Aspects

Established in 2017

The Sables Trust was founded to specifically support the national men's rugby team, providing a dedicated organization focused on their success.

Focus on Player Welfare

The Trust addresses issues related to the well-being of the players, ensuring they have the necessary resources and support both on and off the field.

Logistical Support

We assist with all logistical aspects of the team, such as travel arrangements, accommodation, and equipment needs for training and competitions.

Team Recognition

The Sables team and its coach, Piet Benade, received recognition at the Annual National Sports Awards (ANSA), highlighting their achievements.

World Cup Qualification

The Trust plays a crucial role in the team's preparations for the Rugby Africa Cup, which is a qualification tournament for the World Cup.
